As EngD at Eindhoven University of Technology my main responsibility was to create an extensive documentation platform around our internal data collection software Data Foundry. This included writing extensive documentation articles for administrators, researchers and students. I was responsible for communication with various stakeholders across multiple institutions, helping these stakeholders implement Data Foundry into their curriculum and courses. 

The documentation included platform tours that guide users through the platform to help them get started with Data Logging and prototyping, an instanced link engine to make links work across Data Foundry instances and various live AI examples.
