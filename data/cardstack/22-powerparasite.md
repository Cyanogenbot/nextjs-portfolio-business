The Power Parasite Project was a Data-Enabled Design research project developed in collaboration with Mercedes-Benz, exploring how people think about and manage energy use through everyday phone charging habits. Data-Enabled Design aims to collect and make sense of data in context. This is done by weaving together collect data with annecdotes creating stories based on lived experiences rather than assumptions.

The project centered around a custom research probe that tracked phone charging data in real time and simulated a future of energy scarcity, prompting participants to reflect on habits they had never had reason to question.
