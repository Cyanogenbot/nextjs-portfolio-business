title: Polaris
url: /images/polaris.jpg

Polaris is a speculative design exploration into creating more awareness around power-use through multimodal ambient interfaces. It was created to help homeowners become more energy independent by helping them stay within their energy generation capacity. The polari can be attached to cables of appliances and monitor their power draw, in case too much power is drawn, they sonify their power use and guide homeowners in their decision which appliances to turn off.
