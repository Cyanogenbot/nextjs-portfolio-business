The Frictious Identity Wallets research project was a design exploration developed in collaboration with SURF, investigating how European identity wallets interfaces can promote thoughtful data-sharing. While identity wallets offer users greater control over their personal data, the challenge lies in designing interactions that encourage mindful sharing rather than mindless consent.

The research experimented with alternate data confirmation interactions, testing how specific UI elements and flow steps influence user awareness during data-sharing moments. The goal was to generate actionable insights on how wallet applications can foster more awareness and intentionality in their data-sharing interactions.
